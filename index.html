Cybersecurity Awareness

🎣 What is Phishing?

Phishing is a cyberattack where criminals impersonate trusted organizations (like your bank, delivery services, or streaming apps) to trick you into revealing sensitive data. They use urgency and fear to make you click fake links or hand over passwords.

🦠 What is Malware?

Malware (malicious software) includes viruses, trojans, and ransomware designed to infiltrate, damage, or steal data from your device. It often hides in suspicious downloads, infected email attachments, or deceptive applications.
