Understanding the threats Mal.exe Denied detects and neutralizes

PHISHING ATTACKS

Phishing attacks trick users into revealing sensitive data by impersonating trusted entities. They use urgency, fear, and authority manipulation to bypass rational decision-making and steal credentials.

3.4BPhishing emails sent daily
36%Of data breaches involve phishing
🦠

MALWARE DELIVERY

Malware is distributed through malicious links, email attachments, and compromised websites. Types include ransomware, trojans, spyware, and cryptominers that damage systems and steal data.

560KNew malware samples daily
94%Delivered via email vectors
🎭

SOCIAL ENGINEERING

Social engineering exploits human psychology rather than technical vulnerabilities. Attackers use pretexting, baiting, and quid pro quo to manipulate victims into compromising their own security.
