Understanding the Threats

Knowledge is your first line of defense.

🎣 What is Phishing?

Phishing is a cyberattack where attackers impersonate trusted organizations (banks, services, bosses) to trick you into revealing sensitive information like passwords or credit card details.

  • Fake bank login pages
  • Urgent password reset emails
  • "You've won a prize" text messages

🦠 What is Malware?

Malware (Malicious Software) is designed to secretly access, damage, or take control of your device. It often hides behind innocent-looking links or attachments.

  • Ransomware: Locks your files until you pay.
  • Spyware: Secretly watches what you type.
  • Trojans: Viruses disguised as real software.
